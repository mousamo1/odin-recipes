Lasagna recipe

Description

This is one of the easiest lasagna recipes there is. It's been passed down from my grandma, and can be done with a few simple ingredients.

Highly recommended, please follow along below for the recipe and ingredients.

Ingredients:
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato passata
  • Squared Pasta
  • Onions
  • Ground beef
  • Carrots (optional)
Directions:
  1. Heat pan over medium high heat
  2. When hot, add onions, your selection of herbs and spices, and simmer
  3. Add ground beef and mix well
  4. When brown, consider it done!
